Setting

After Vikter carried out the glorious evolution, upon Ekko's failure, Piltover and Zaun were successfully evolved. Except, instead of becoming the same soldier-like forms that was Ambessa's army, they still kept a bit of themselves. Each individual was still unique based on their characteristics and personality. Viktor went back to Zaun with evolved Jayce to keep trying to improve the nation that he basically now ruled.
Uriel was a pilty that had origins from Zaun, much like Vikter. He grew up with kat & was often protected by them in the past. But as Uriel strove for more in the glistening buildings of Piltover, Kat didn't see the same vision. She thought ill of Piltover like almost every other zaunite, and rightfully so.
