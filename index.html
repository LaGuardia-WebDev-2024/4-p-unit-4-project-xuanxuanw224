In Our Timeline

Setting

After Vikter was actually successful in accomplishing his glorious evolution, (Ekko failed :c), much of Piltover and Zaun became evolved. Except, instead of becoming the same soldier-like forms that was Ambessa's army, they still kept a bit of themselves, each individually unique based on a person's characteristics and personality. Viktor went back to zaun with evolved Jayce to keep trying to improve the nation that he basically now rules.
Uriel was a pilty that had origins from Zaun, much like Vikter. He grew up with kat & was often protected by them in the past. But as Uriel strove for more in the glistening buildings of Piltover, Kat didn't see the same vision. She thought ill of Piltover like almost every other zaunite, and rightfully so.
